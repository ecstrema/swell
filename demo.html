Feature Overview

The timeline component is now treated as a signal that appears in the signal list, just like regular waveform signals.

  • Timeline appears as the first signal by default
  • Multiple timelines can be added
  • Each timeline has independent zoom/pan controls
  • "Add Timeline" button at the bottom of the signal list
